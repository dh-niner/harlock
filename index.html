Abyssopelagic Zone


Depth range: 4000 to 6000 meters

Temperature range: 32° to 39°F or 0 to 4°C

Found below the bathypelagic zone and above the hadopelagic zone.

Relevant threat: deep-sea mining

Important/unique zone feature: The coldest zone

Fun Fact: The name (abyss) comes from a Greek word meaning "no bottom" because they thought the ocean was bottomless.
